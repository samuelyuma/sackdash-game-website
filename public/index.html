About This Game

The making of this game was inspired by one of the traditional games that were often contested during the independence day celebrations, namely "sack racing". This game was created to provide a fun playing experience for all ages and can be accessed easily through the website. With attractive pixel-shaped visuals and simple gameplay, we hope this game will entertain each of you!
